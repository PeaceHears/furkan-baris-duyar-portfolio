About Me

A passionate game programmer with commercial experience at studios like Masomo/Miniclip, contributing to flagship mobile titles played by over 110 million players. My journey began at university, building games in Unity/C#, and has since evolved to mastering C++ with Cocos2d-x and now Unreal Engine 5.

I graduated with an MSc in Computer Games Technology from Abertay University, UK, where I dived deep into advanced graphics with DirectX11 and AI-driven procedural content generation. I thrive on solving complex technical challenges and building immersive gaming experiences from the ground up.
